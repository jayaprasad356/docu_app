What Is Online Legal Consultation?

Online Legal consultation or online Lawyer consultation is a method to connect Users and Lawyers virtually. It is a convenient and easy way to get online Lawyer advice using Our Service.

When To File A Consumer Complaints?

The Consumer Complaint is required to filed with the concerned consumer forum having jurisdiction within 2 years from which the consumer has become aware of the defective goods or of deficient services, as the case may be.

What Is The Time Limit To Resolve A Complaint?

Our panel advocates take suitable legal action as soon as they receive all the details from the consumer. The resolution depends on the concerned company's responses to the legal action.

How Consumer Challenged Court Decision?

The consumer can challenge the decision of the concerned tribunal to State Commission/National Commission/Supreme Court depending on the forum before which the complaint is first filed.

How Do Customer Make The Payment To File A Case?
